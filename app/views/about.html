Story

When I was doing my undergrad in Computer Science and Engineering one of my professors asked me build a site for an conference that was held in our college. It all started from there, I started loving designing websites and my CS course work gave me required skills to put my designs live. My genuine interest in understand people pushed me completely into designing products for that solves their day to day problems using design and tech. Seeing my passion my professor recommended me for different design project they came across. That led me to do many freelance projects and even run my own web design agency for a period. Taking my experience in design, I joining Web 2.0 Innovation labs, TCS Chennai as UX Designer. There I contributed to research, design, prototype, as well as front-end development for 3 years. However, I wanted to get more formal education in the user-centered design process for creating enhanced user experience. With this in mind, I joined Human-Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE) MS program at the University of Washington.
