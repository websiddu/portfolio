Summary

As part of my part time job at University of Washington. I solely designed and developed a portal for the department of Environmental stewardship and sustainability. I rebuilt the whole system from scratch discarding what they had already.

Project Links
  1. Demo of the portal
  2. Source Code

Green Certification Programs at the University of Washington are recognition programs intended to reduce the environmental impact of various spaces across campus by educating students, faculty, and staff about sustainable practices. The programs provide an evaluation framework with resources to implement the practices within their spaces, in alignment with strategies laid out in the UW Climate Action Plan.

Process

Though this project has less budget, throughout the project I followed the User Centered Design process. This includes lot of ideation, paper prototyping, usability testing followed by developing the product.

Research

Before jumping into the prototyping, I did some user research to get some inputs form the stackholders. I did 5 interviews with the people who use the product. I didn't use a survey as the user group is really small. Next I conducted a focus group to get some inputs from the people who use this products.
