The Problem

Department of Sustainability at University of Washington has a green certification program that certifies the 4500 labs/offices and more than 6000 sorority houses around the campus based on a set of questions.

So the problem is to motivate labs, offices, and sorority houses representatives to take the certification program and make survey taking and creating experience better & non-frustrating. In short, I was hired to improve their current experience of the certification portal.

Summary

I solely designed and developed a portal for the department of sustainability. I ended up redesigning the whole system from scratch discarding what they had already.

Green Certification Programs at the University of Washington are recognition programs intended to reduce the environmental impact of various spaces across campus by educating students, faculty, and staff about sustainable practices. The programs provide an evaluation framework with resources to implement the practices within their spaces, in alignment with strategies laid out in the UW Climate Action Plan.

Process

Though this project has less budget, throughout the project I followed the User Centered Design process. This includes lot of ideation, paper prototyping, usability testing followed by developing the product.

Research

Before jumping into the prototyping, I did some user research to get some inputs form the stackholders. I did ~4 interviews with the people who use the product. I didn't use a survey as the user group is small. Next I conducted a focus group to get some inputs from the people who use this products.
